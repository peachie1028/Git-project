Laufey

Laufey is a gentle type of vocalist, she is an icelandic-chinese singer with a modern jazz style. Laufey is not just a talented singer but also a talented musician with beautiful instrument play.

Laufey is comparable if not better in vocals than conan. She is one of the best musician between the 8 main characters. She is also the nicest and most social out of the 8, She is friends with everyone in the NJL.
